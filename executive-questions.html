Independent candidate brief by Russell Dudek

Executive Interview Questions

Questions designed to expose the real mandate, decision architecture, technical operating model and success conditions.

The architecture follows the answers - not the job title.
Russell Dudek | Executive interview questions1

Questions 01-08 | Mandate, production and authority

01 | Mandate and sponsorship

What business outcome caused this role to be opened now, beyond the general acceleration of AI?

02 | Portfolio

Where does AI demand enter, and who has authority to fund, sequence, standardize or stop it?

03 | Production boundary

What evidence currently separates an experiment, pilot and production service?

04 | Guest consequence

Which guest or team-member moments carry the greatest opportunity and the highest tolerance constraints?

05 | Architecture

Which data, identity, cloud, device, integration or observability boundaries most constrain scale?

06 | Human authority

How are override, escalation and stop rights designed for AI-assisted operational decisions?

07 | Testing

What are the required SIT, E2E, regression, performance, security and recovery expectations by risk tier?

08 | Operating model

How are data science, ML engineering, platform, product, cyber, privacy, legal and operations accountable together after launch?

Russell Dudek | Executive interview questions2

Questions 09-16 | Team, partners, economics and trust

09 | Team

Where is the team strongest today, and which capabilities must this director recruit, develop or reorganize?

10 | Partners

Which vendors or APIs create strategic leverage, and which create unacceptable concentration or continuity risk?

11 | Economics

How are ROI, cost-to-serve, platform reuse and operating benefits measured and challenged?

12 | Adoption

What has made prior technology changes stick - or fail to stick - with frontline and enterprise users?

13 | Responsible AI

Which privacy, fairness, explainability or regulatory questions are already shaping the roadmap?

14 | Incident learning

How are model, data, system and workflow incidents reviewed and converted into reusable controls?

15 | Year-one success

What would make the first year unmistakably successful to the CIO, park operations and the executive team?

16 | Objection

What concern about my background would you want resolved before trusting me with this mandate?

Listen for where ownership is implicit, evidence is fragmented, production responsibility changes hands, or the guest consequence is not visible in the technical decision.
